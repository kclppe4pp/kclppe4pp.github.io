Graduate Conference

PPE for Public Policy 2025

Wed · 26 Nov 2025Waterloo Campus · FWB Room1.11London, UKIn-person

This one day conference aims to bring together Ph.D. students working at the intersections of Philosophy, Politics, and Economics to address pressing policy issues, and to receive constructive feedback from peers and faculty members in the School of Politics and Economics at King’s. Our keynote speaker is Professor Jonathan Wolff.

Call for Abstracts

We invited submissions from Ph.D. students in all areas of PPE (broadly construed) that aim at contributing to public policy. Cross-disciplinary and cross-methodological work is welcome.

  • Presentation: 15 minutes + 5–10 minutes Q&A.
  • Abstract requirements: anonymised abstract ≤ 400 words.
  • Catering: free coffee breaks and lunch provided by the KCL PPE Research Group.
